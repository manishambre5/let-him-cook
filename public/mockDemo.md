# Broccoli and Cheese Frittata  
*Quick, easy, and makes a satisfying meal with just a few ingredients.*  

---

### **Ingredients**
- **Your ingredients:**
  - 4 eggs  
  - 1 cup broccoli florets, chopped  
  - 1 tablespoon butter  

- **Additional ingredients:**
  - ¼ cup shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or Parmesan)  
  - 2 tablespoons milk or water (optional, for fluffier eggs)  
  - Salt and black pepper to taste  
  - 1 tablespoon olive oil or extra butter (for cooking)  

---

### **Instructions**
1. **Prep the broccoli:**  
   Steam or blanch the broccoli until just tender (about 3–4 minutes). Drain and set aside.  

2. **Whisk the eggs:**  
   In a bowl, whisk the eggs with milk (if using), salt, and pepper until well combined. Stir in half of the shredded cheese.  

3. **Cook:**  
   In an oven-safe nonstick skillet (about 8–10 inches), melt the butter with olive oil over medium heat. Add the cooked broccoli and sauté for 1 minute.  

4. **Add eggs:**  
   Pour the egg mixture over the broccoli, tilting the pan to spread evenly. Reduce heat to medium-low and cook without stirring until edges set, about 3–4 minutes. Sprinkle the remaining cheese on top.  

5. **Finish cooking:**  
   If your skillet is oven-safe, transfer it to a preheated broiler (or bake at 400°F/200°C) for 2–3 minutes until the top is golden and the center is set. *Alternatively*, cover the skillet and cook on low heat for another 4–5 minutes until fully set.  

6. **Serve:**  
   Slide the frittata onto a plate, slice into wedges, and enjoy warm.  

---

### **Optional Additions**  
- Add diced onions or garlic if you have them.  
- Top with avocado, salsa, or fresh herbs.  
- Serve with toast or a side salad.  

*This recipe is flexible — you can easily double it or add other veggies you have on hand.*
